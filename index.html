My name is Vaibhav Thakur, I completed my Bacholer of Science (BS) and Master of Science (MS) degrees in Neuroscience, at Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, India. Additionally, I also hold Master of Arts (MA) in Psychology from University of California, Los Angeles.

I am currently working towards my doctorate in Prof. Michele Basso's lab on the interface between learning and perceptual decision making in mice using cutting-edge techologies such as electrophysiology and optogenetics. I am also work on decision-making in humans especially in patients with Parkinson's disease (PD) and try to understand how PD affects decision-making ability in humans.

I am an eternal learner, a very result-oriented person, and my interests are in machine learning, deep learning (especially in Computer Vision, and NLP), with a special crush on Healthcare.

I am currently a research intern at Mila Quebec AI Institute under the supervisions of Yoshua Bengio and Dianbo Lui. I am also a Pharma Technical Analyst, and member of the Future Labs at Roche Canada, and African NLP Researcher at Masakhane.

I am the co-creator of the FFRTranslate, of Speech Recognition Model for Fon, and Okwugbe ASR Python library.
